About the Website
Growers Planning Solutions is a fictitious company, designed for the purpose of making a really cool logo whose acronym is the same as the site. The site was prototyped for the Lake Washington Institute of Technologies DreamWeaver class, DSGN 233. At the moment it runs on a rudimentary file system, but it was built so that changing to a database is in alignment with the design. The purpose of the website was to have everything the average gardener needs to know about plants that they are likely to grow. It is of assistance in purchasing and landscaping decisions, without running all over the Internet seeking answers. The user can pick a type of plant on the main page, and then display different types of information about that plant on any of several pages. The Gardener's Planting System website is also available at www.ormer.net/gps.
